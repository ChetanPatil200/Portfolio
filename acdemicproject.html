Automatic light intensity controlled by external light sensing. (04/2021 - 08/2021)

* It helps in automatically switching ON and OFF of the light based on external light intensity.

* The main purpose of this project is to minimize the cost & loss of electricity and also manpower to manually turned on and off of the street light. After implementing the Project it should be capable of emitting the light when there is darkness around it.

* The objective of this work is to design a device that will automatically controls light intensity. The proposed device controls street light intensity by calculating the peak hours. This work explains light controlling using Arduino UNO.

* After the implementation of the project, it produces ambident light depending upon the surrounding intensity of light. When there is a darkness around the sensor it produces a light and when the light increases the LED bulbs slowly turned off.

Automatic Hand Sanatizer. (02/2020 - 09/2020)

* The project is capable of sensing hand and it dispenses out the sanatizer when there is contact of hand near the transparent pipe. The power supply provides the voltage and current for the operation of motor and board and its power source is battery and the power supply can be recharged.

* The main advantage is that we don't need any Physical pressure to be applied on it.

* It is environmental friendly as because of the disposal wastages is very minimal, and it can be refilled easily without any technical assistances.
